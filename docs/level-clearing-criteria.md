# Level Clearing Criteria

Each stage in PebbleSum has specific criteria a student must meet to advance to the next stage.

## General Rules

1. **Practice Requirement** — Complete the required number of practice sessions before attempting Level Clear.
2. **Perfect Score** — Answer 100% of questions correctly in the Level Clear test.
3. **Time Limit** — Complete the Level Clear test within the allotted time.

All three conditions must be met simultaneously to advance.

---

## Foundation Stages (Practice-Then-Pass)

These stages build number recognition and counting fluency. They require more practice repetitions before attempting the test.

| Stage | Name | Practice Sessions Required | Level Clear Questions | Time Limit | Problem Style |
|-------|------|---------------------------|----------------------|------------|---------------|
| 6A | Know Your Numbers | 5 | 10 | 30s | Multiple Choice |
| 5A | Count to 10 | 5 | 10 | 30s | Multiple Choice |
| 4A | Count to 30 | 5 | 10 | 30s | Multiple Choice |
| 3A | Number Sequence | 5 | 10 | 30s | Multiple Choice |

### Criteria for Foundation Stages

- Complete **5 practice sessions** on the stage
- In Level Clear: answer all **10 questions correctly** (zero errors allowed)
- Finish within **30 seconds** (3 seconds per question)

---

## Computation Stages (Timed-Pass)

These stages test arithmetic fluency. They require fewer practice sessions but demand speed and accuracy.

| Stage | Name | Practice Sessions Required | Level Clear Questions | Time Limit | Problem Style |
|-------|------|---------------------------|----------------------|------------|---------------|
| 2A | Add Small (+1 to +3) | 3 | 15 | 45s | Numpad |
| A | Add to 10 | 3 | 15 | 45s | Numpad |
| B | Add to 20 | 3 | 15 | 45s | Numpad |
| C | Subtract Small (within 10) | 3 | 15 | 45s | Numpad |
| D | Subtract to 20 | 3 | 15 | 45s | Numpad |
| E | Multiply (2, 5, 10) | 3 | 15 | 45s | Numpad |
| F | Divide (2, 5, 10) | 3 | 15 | 45s | Numpad |

### Criteria for Computation Stages

- Complete **3 practice sessions** on the stage
- In Level Clear: answer all **15 questions correctly** (zero errors allowed)
- Finish within **45 seconds** (3 seconds per question)

---

## Additional Game Mechanics

### Hearts System
- Players start each session with **5 hearts**
- Each wrong answer costs 1 heart
- If hearts reach 0, the session ends early (failed attempt)
- Hearts do not carry between sessions

### XP Rewards (Practice Mode Only)
- Base: `correct answers x 10` XP
- Perfect bonus: +50 XP (all questions correct)
- Speed bonus: +25 XP (completed within Standard Completion Time)
- Failed Level Clear attempts award **no XP**

### Standard Completion Time (SCT)
The SCT is the target time for regular practice sessions (not Level Clear). It's used for the speed bonus calculation:
- Foundation stages: 180–240 seconds
- Computation stages: 180–240 seconds

---

## Progression Flow

```
[Practice x N sessions] → [Unlock Level Clear] → [Pass: 100% + within time] → [Next Stage]
                                                   ↓
                                              [Fail] → [Keep practicing, retry anytime]
```

A failed Level Clear does not reset practice count — the student can retry immediately.

---

## World Groupings

Stages are grouped into themed worlds for the Journey Map:

1. **Pebble Meadow** — 6A, 5A (number basics)
2. **Number Forest** — 4A, 3A (bigger numbers)
3. **Addition River** — 2A, A, B (addition mastery)
4. **Subtraction Cave** — C, D (subtraction mastery)
5. **Multiply Castle** — E (multiplication)
6. **Division Galaxy** — F (division)

Completing all stages in a world gives a sense of "world complete" before moving on.
